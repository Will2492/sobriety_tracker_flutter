# SobriOK Subscription & Monetization

This document defines the subscription model and monetization rules for SobriOK.

---

## Subscription model

- Type: Auto-renewable subscription
- Duration: Monthly
- Price: €9.99 / month
- Platforms: App Store, Google Play
- Cancellation: Anytime via store settings

SobriOK can be fully used without a subscription.

---

## Free features (always available)

- Sobriety duration tracking
- Start date storage
- Reset / relapse flow
- Milestones (90 / 180 / 270 / 365)
- Local data storage
- Language selection (English / Russian)
- Offline functionality

---

## Premium features (subscription)

Premium provides additional insight and structure.  
It never unlocks or blocks core functionality.

### Premium includes

- Advanced progress analytics
- Pattern and trend insights
- Money and time saved calculations
- Daily intention and reflection flow
- Cloud backup (future)
- Enhanced milestone summaries
- Additional Telegram-based bonuses

---

## Premium access rules

- No feature essential to sobriety is blocked
- Milestones are always accessible
- Premium only enhances understanding and reflection
- No pressure messaging

---

## Paywall principles

- Shown only when accessing Premium features
- Never forced on first launch
- Always includes "Continue free" option
- Clear explanation of value and price

---

## Required legal text

### English

€9.99 per month.  
Subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.  
You can manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store or Google Play account settings.

### Russian

9,99 € в месяц.  
Подписка продлевается автоматически, если не отменена минимум за 24 часа до окончания текущего периода.  
Управление подпиской и отмена доступны в настройках App Store или Google Play.

## Ethics & compliance

- No medical claims
- No promises of results
