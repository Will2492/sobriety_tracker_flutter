## Full description

### English

SobriOK is a calm, ad-free companion designed to help you stay aware of your sobriety journey — without pressure or judgment.

The app focuses on clarity, consistency, and honest self-reflection. It does not promise results or make medical claims.

**What SobriOK offers:**
- Clear sobriety tracking (days, hours, minutes)
- Optional daily intentions and reflections
- Milestones every 90 days to mark meaningful points in time
- Local-first privacy — your data stays with you
- Manual language selection (English / Russian)

**Milestones & appreciation**  
SobriOK gently acknowledges meaningful milestones on your path.  
Every 90 days, you may receive a small digital message or reflection — optional and pressure-free.

**Premium (optional subscription)**  
SobriOK offers an optional monthly subscription (€9.99/month) that unlocks deeper insights and reflection tools.  
The core tracking experience is always free.

Sobriety is a process.  
SobriOK walks with you — one step at a time.
